About Us

elite food supplies is highly regarded for its integrity & ethical culture and has a sterling reputation within the industry.

We are a proud Australian Company specializing in the wholesale and distribution of Food Consumable Packaging, Washroom & Hygiene, Hospitality and Food Service Products.

Our commitment and passion is in supporting the supply of environmentally sustainable products to the ever-changing marketplace. We strongly advocate and are committed to sourcing waste-reducing materials to enhance product sustainability.

At elite food supplies we believe that we have a social and moral obligation to supply products that serve to preserve and protect the environment.
